* Ian Myoungsu Choi
* Tony Narongkrit

* Rosna Thomas
* Parth Bhasin

* Mounica Nadu M

Slides for the presentation can be found [here](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1PDW7ngo_H_vB1tMk6MVvk7n4iOuVxn-Jk3k61RBTXws). Big thank you to the presenters.
